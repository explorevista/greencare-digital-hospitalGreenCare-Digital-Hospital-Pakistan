Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

GreenCare Digital Hospital Pakistan ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our booking services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, telephone number, and address that you voluntarily give to us when you register an account or book an appointment.
  • Booking Information: Details regarding your medical appointment preferences, selected doctor, and payment status.
  • Derivative Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs.

3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you to:

  • Process and manage your medical appointments.
  • Send you appointment confirmations and reminders via WhatsApp or Email.
  • Process payments and maintain transaction records.
  • Improve our website and services.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations. Your information may be disclosed to third-party doctors to facilitate your appointment. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers.

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.
